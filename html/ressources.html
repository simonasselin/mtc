Ressources

Below is a list of recommended books on typography compiled by former TDC President Charles Nix. For more in depth reviews on a select number of publications, please see our Book Reviews section. We will be continually posting reviews of upcoming titles and if you’re an author and would like your book considered for review by the TDC, contact us to send a copy along for review.

By clicking on these links and purchasing a book you will be helping to support the TDC thanks to the magic of Amazon Associates. To recommend a book that you love for our list, please contact us and we’ll be happy to consider it.
